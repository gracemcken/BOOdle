BOOdle

How to Play
Guess the BOOdle in 6 tries! It's Wordle with a spooky twist! Click the toggle to the right of the rules button to listen to some mysterious music and guess what the Halloween themed word is! After each guess the colour of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was.
  • Green: You got the right letter in the right position!
  • Yellow: You've got one of the letters, just not in the right spot!
  • Grey: Uh oh! This letter isn't in the word!
If you want to try a new word, simply refresh the page and start again!
