BOOdle

How to Play
Guess the BOOdle in 6 tries! It's Wordle with a spooky twist! Guess what the Halloween themed word is!
After each guess the colour of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was.
  • Green: You got the right letter in the right position!
  • Yellow: You've got one of the letters, just not in the right spot!
  • Grey: Uh oh! This letter isn't in the word!
